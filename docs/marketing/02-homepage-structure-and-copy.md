- **Copy:** Use Option 1 from above. "Stop posting Zerodha trades manually."

### The Visual / Social Proof
- **Goal:** Build immediate trust.
- **Copy:** "Trusted by CAs and accountants to eliminate hours of manual journal entries per month." Visual flow: `[Zerodha CSV] -> [TradeBooks AI Engine] -> [Tally XML]`

### The Problem
- **Headline:** The manual posting bottleneck.
- **Copy:** "Zerodha gives you raw data. Tally needs structured journal entries. Bridging that gap manually with spreadsheets costs hours per client, and exposes you to errors that only surface during audits." 
- **Bullet points:** Manual entry time drain, spreadsheet fragility, audit season scramble.

### How It Works
- **Headline:** 4 steps to Tally-ready books.
- **Step 1:** Upload (Drop your Zerodha tradebook, funds, and holdings exports. We start with the files you already have.)
- **Step 2:** Configure (Select Investor or Trader mode. Set your Tally company context and ledger mappings.)
- **Step 3:** Reconcile (Let the engine cross-verify entries and surface any mismatches. Review exceptions before they ever reach Tally.)
- **Step 4:** Export (Download your Tally-importable XML and import it directly into Tally Prime or ERP 9.)

### Benefits / Features
- **Headline:** Built for accounting precision, not just data parsing.
- **Feature 1:** Reconcile before import (Spot discrepancies early).
- **Feature 2:** Investor & Trader logic (Accurate tax treatment).
- **Feature 3:** Traceable audit trail (Every entry maps to a source row).
- **Feature 4:** Tally-native output (Zero manual adjustments needed post-export).

### Who It's For

- **Primary:** CA Firms (Manage multiple client books efficiently).
- **Secondary:** Accounting Teams (Standardize SME processes).
- **Secondary:** Self-Filers/Active Traders (Clean books for easier filing).

## 5. FAQ Section & Priority Questions

**Headline:** Frequently Asked Questions

1. **Q: Do I need to learn a new accounting system?**
   **A:** No. TradeBooks AI is a secure bridge between your existing broker exports from Zerodha and your current Tally setup.
2. **Q: Can I trust the generated entries to be accurate?**
   **A:** Yes. We prioritize reconciliation before export. The tool highlights exceptions and mismatches, giving you full control to review before you import into Tally.
3. **Q: Does this handle Investor (Capital Gains) vs Trader (Business Income) classifications?**
   **A:** Yes, you can configure the engine for either mode depending on the client's profile.
4. **Q: Will it work with brokers other than Zerodha?**
   **A:** Currently, we are heavily optimized for Zerodha to ensure the highest quality parsing and reconciliation. Support for other brokers is not in V1.
5. **Q: Do I have to map ledgers manually every time?**
   **A:** No, you configure your Tally company context and ledger mappings once, and the system applies them to your exports.
